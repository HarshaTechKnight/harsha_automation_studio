Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our automation services

How long does it take to implement automation?

Most workflows are deployed within 1-2 weeks. Simple integrations can go live in 3-5 days, while complex enterprise systems may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

What platforms do you integrate with?

We specialize in 10+ platforms including Shopify, Slack, Google Workspace (Sheets, Gmail, Drive, Calendar), Trello, Asana, Notion, HubSpot, and custom APIs. If a platform has an API or webhook capability, we can integrate it.

How much does automation cost?

Pricing depends on complexity and scope. Simple workflows start at ₹75,000, while comprehensive automation systems range from ₹1,75,000-₹4,00,000+. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All projects include 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer monthly maintenance packages that include monitoring, optimization, and updates as your business evolves.

What if my business process is unique?

That's our specialty! We build custom automation solutions tailored to your specific business processes. Every workflow is designed from scratch based on your unique requirements and existing tools.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We design integrations that work seamlessly with your current tech stack. Whether you use custom software, legacy systems, or modern SaaS tools, we can connect them.

What ROI can we expect?

Most clients see ROI within 3-6 months. Our average client saves 20+ hours per week and reduces errors by 90%+. We provide detailed ROI calculations during the consultation phase.

Do you offer training for our team?

Yes! We provide comprehensive documentation and can conduct training workshops (₹25,000 per session) to help your team understand and manage the automated workflows.
